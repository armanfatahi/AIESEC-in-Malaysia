Malaysia Youth to Business
The Malaysia Youth to Business Forum (Y2B) is an unique event which connects top young leaders of the country with global business and social leaders, to explore entrepreneurial and current issues related to the world of technology, business and living while showcasing the impact we can have on the world through our positive actions. In Jan 2013, this event was highly successful with an attendance of over 600 delegates from major universities in Malaysia.
LEAD Conference
LEAD Conference aims to gather the leaders of Malaysia in and out of AIESEC. The objectives are to foster connection between AIESEC Leaders and high profile speakers/organisations to provide extraordinary insight and profound inspiration. Successful and high profile speakers from various industries will be delivering topics and sharing personal experiences aligned with the theme of the conference to inspire the Malaysian youth leaders in striving further for growth.
