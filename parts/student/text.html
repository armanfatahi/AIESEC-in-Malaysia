Volunteering abroad on
              issue-based development projects
An international volunteer abroad programme for Malaysian youths to travel across the world to live a cross-cultural learning experience while working in projects based on specific social issues and NGO's that aim to create a positive impact in society.
A self-discovery and self-development experience. We invite you to embrace the world together with AIESEC.



































Personal & Professional Challenge in

AIESEC's Global Internship Programme helps you to find a placement in an organization in another country for 2-18 months.
