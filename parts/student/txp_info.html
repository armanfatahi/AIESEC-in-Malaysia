To increase youth impact in the world, we see that society needs leaders who are entrepreneurial, culturally sensitive, socially responsible and take an active part in their own learning. To gain a holistic experience AIESEC offers you the opportunity to take on a leadership role on top of the opportunities for international volunteering and internships. Learn how to lead a team, manage large projects and run a local or national organization along with people from all over the world!

Each year, AIESEC provides over 86,000 of its members the challenging opportunity to support the management of the global association through our leadership development programmes. There is a powerful connection that is created by working towards a common vision with people from such diverse backgrounds. The leadership programmes offer the opportunity at a young age for you to experience executive-level responsibilities, develop yourself personally & professionally as well as meet & work with like-minded people that share your values from around the world.

  • How to Apply:  Apply through your local office
